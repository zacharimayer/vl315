Est. 1953

Rooted in Miami

Village Lodge No. 315, F&AM, was chartered on April 22, 1953, in the close‑knit community of Miami Shores. Since its founding, the lodge has served as a center of fellowship, moral development, and service for generations of Masons.

Over seven decades, Village Lodge 315 has upheld the timeless principles of Freemasonry while adapting to the evolving needs of its members and the wider community.
